Director

William J. Stribling is an independent filmmaker, director, and screenwriter working across narrative film, documentary, and interactive media. His work spans feature films, music videos, and documentaries, with a focus on character-driven storytelling and visually distinct worlds.

He currently teaches feature screenwriting at Chapman University, where he is a highly sought-after adjunct professor known for his practical, industry-focused approach. His courses consistently receive top student evaluations, and his sections regularly fill with writers seeking a more rigorous and production-oriented education.

In addition to his film work, Stribling writes for video games, collaborating with top-tier industry talent, including the lead writer of Helldivers 2. He also mentors writers and producers at all levels, helping develop projects from early concept through production.

Stribling's work as both a working filmmaker and educator places him at the intersection of craft and execution, with a focus on preparing storytellers for the realities of today's evolving entertainment landscape.
